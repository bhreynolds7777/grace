Who We Are

Grace Is a Gift Meant to Be Shared

Giving Grace is a faith-inspired non-profit dedicated to meeting people where they are — in the gaps between struggle and stability, between despair and hope.

We believe that generosity is contagious. When one person extends grace to another, the ripple effect can transform families, neighborhoods, and entire communities. That's why everything we do starts with a simple act: giving.

  • Transparent, accountable stewardship of every donation
  • Direct, relationship-based service delivery
  • Rooted in dignity, equity, and compassion
  • Partnering with local churches and community organizations

Get Involved

Give Your Time & Talents

You don't need a lot to make a difference — just a willing heart. Volunteers are the hands and feet of Giving Grace.

  • Pantry Volunteer

    Sort donations, pack boxes, and distribute food and goods to families in need.

  • Tutor / Mentor

    Spend time with youth or adults who need academic support or life skills coaching.

  • Skills-Based Volunteer

    Offer your professional skills — legal, medical, financial, marketing, or tech — on a pro-bono basis.

  • Church / Group Partner

    Rally your congregation or group to serve together through drives, projects, or mission days.
